What is XR?

Augmented Reality - Courtesy of Magic Leap

Extended Reality (XR), is made up of three components: VR, AR, & MR.

In a Virtual Reality (VR) experience, the user is completely in a digital world.

Augmented Reality (AR) is an interactive experience that combines the real world and digital world.

Mixed Reality (MR) combines the real world and the digital world in a way that makes the real world interact with the digital world.

Our studio will utilize AR and MR for our games, as opposed to VR.
