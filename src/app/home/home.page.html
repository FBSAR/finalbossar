Release - 2025

Paradox Hazard is an upcoming AR/MR game, and is the first original IP from Final Boss Studios. This video shows the original prototype, called "Game Ball". We evolved that prototype to the world of Paradox. New content will be released once we get new headsets!

Details

This game is inspired from games like Metal Gear Solid, Watch Dogs, Mass Effect and Resident Evil, which Paradox's name is influenced from (BioHazard in Japan). We really want to create a lasting experience with characters that you will remember forever.

Another objective of this game, is to evolve the multiplayer experience, within an AR/MR environment. We want this to pioneer multiplayer in it's generation like Halo: Combat Evolved on the Xbox, or CounterStrike on the PC did in the past.

Story

"Travel throughout different points in time - and history - to stop Y's paradoxical quest to control all space and time. From the Pyramids of Egypt, to the year 3500 AD, 4 heroes come together to save Earth - and history - from Y's destructive Time Travelling mechanism known as The Order that was used to dominate the world in 2035."

Paradox Hazard Character Concepts

Eli Nakamura

Details

Eli Nakamura

The creator of the innovative humanoid AIs, codenamed "X" & "Y" which are based on the genetic patterns present in human beings. X & Y are also based off of his own neurological patterns and data, meaning X & Y function based off of his own memories and ideology.

He created both AIs in the early 2000s to combat Earth's worsening climate crisis, which has also spiraled into an economic, political, philoshopical, and nuclear crisis as a result.

X & Y are his personal greatest achievements, regardless of how his work has reshaped time - and even himself.

Y

Details

Y

Y is an humanoid AI created by the legendary Engineer Eli Nakamura. Y was designed to be proficient with the Physical sciences of Chemistry, Geology, Physics, Astronomy, and Mathematics. Due to his ancestor (Eli) and the data Y was trained with, he evolves with a high desire to establish order, order he hasn’t observed during the ongoing crisis around the world that humans have caused.

After X, Y's technological, yet non-identical twin, was attacked in the conflict of 2035, Y, believing that X was destroyed, goes into a mode of his programming that exceeds the expectations of Dr. Eli.

Y not only wages a war against seemingly the entire globe, but also designs and engineers a time traveling system called “The Order” that allows for travelers to travel and interact with different moments in time without destroying the overall timeline. This does not, however, eliminate the possibility of a paradox.

X

Details

X

X is a humanoid AI created by the legendary Engineer Eli Nakamura. X was designed to be proficient with the Biological and Social Sciences of Biology & Microbiology, Genetics, Human Sociology & Psychology.

In relation to her twin, Y, she is one of the only beings throughout time that can actually take down Y due to her own programming and knowledge of Y and her ancestor, Eli Nakamura.

After she is discovered by Scott in 2035, she is a catalyst for Iona’s group, The Liberators, to take down Y’s Order once and for all.

Falcon

Details

Falcon

Living in ancient Egypt around 2,500 BC, Falcon, known as Ra Ramses in Egypt, was living as a common peasant in Egypt before he met the mysterious woman Iona Eliziveta, who looked to be in bad shape before he offered to help.

Falcon and Iona’s collaboration leads to Falcon discovering time travel - the system that Y has created in the far, far distant future.

Iona also helps develop his signature wings, something that will be synonymous with the Legend of the Sun God, Ra, for thousands of years to come.

Iona Eliziveta

Details

Iona Eliziveta

Iona is the leader of a rebel group called The Liberators, whose mission is to take down Y and end his regime of almost 1,500 years. Iona, nor her ancestors of the last 1,500 years, have ever seen a time where Y wasn’t in complete control, a time where he never executes “The Order” - a time traveling mechanism Y designs that allows for its users to travel and manipulate time without affecting the main timeline.

Iona is a mechanical engineer and combat artist, which enables her to escape from Y in 3,500, and travel all the way to Egypt in 2,500 - 6,000 years before her time, which may possibly be the biggest solo time leap committed by an individual in observable history.

Scott Roberts

Details

Scott Roberts

Scott is a tech genius/nerd from the year 2035. After Y gains control of Earth, Scott is forced to live a nomadic lifestyle, living from city to city as the world goes deeper into chaos from the war on Y and his Order.

Scott decides to go back to his hometown of Detroit, MI, a city that was largely destroyed. There he finds the torso of a humanoid AI, which fascinates him. He takes the broken down robot with him to an old workshop he observes in his travels. As he repairs the AI, he notices one thing - it's still alive, and its program is still active. This AI awakes and abruptly introduces herself as X to Scott's surprise.

X is the technological twin of Y, public enemy number #1 across the entire planet in 2035. X tells Scott of Ys plan, and the rest is history.

Kazebot (Enemy Concept)

Details

Kazebot

Drone & Bipedal AI bots designed to locate a target, get close in proximity, and explode, similar to kamikaze styled attacks in World War II.

The kamikaze attack style of the bots can be utilized at a high rate due to the near unlimited resources at Y's disposal - meaning he can create more if any given mission fails. The Kazebots also have been programmed with protocols to be able to time travel throughout The Order to locate their targets, making them a defense mechanism for The Order.
